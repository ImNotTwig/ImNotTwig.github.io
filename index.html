Me

Hello! my name is Twig, or Lydia, or any variation thereof, eg: Ly, Lyd, or Lyds.
I'm basically a cat in like, real life (but mainly the internet) :3
Also, this is a very basic fact a lot of people seem to miss, I'm a (she/her)

I like Linux, programming, math, and other tech related stuff, though in the grand scheme of things, I'm probably in the pit of despair in terms of knowledge about them. see this image.

Currently I am on NixOS, which is a declarative Linux distro that offers reproducible builds of the parts of your computer it manages, which theoretically could be everything, but usually isnt.

I have around 5+ years of experience with Linux, and I have been programming for about 3. As a child I would program in Scratch for fun, much like a modern developer, I took other's code and incorporated it into my own. Nowadays, I generally program in Zig, Go, or Rust. Sometimes I like dabble in functional languages like Haskell and Ocaml. Of course, I also use nix, because I mean, I use NixOS.

I also enjoy making digital art. I don't really post my art online, but perhaps this site will be where I end up doing so. I mostly draw my own characters, but sometimes i draw other things such as other people's characters or architecture.
